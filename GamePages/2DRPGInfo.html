A Deep Dive

A bullet hell game made in Unity using only mouse input.

Project Overview

A bullet hell game I made in Unity for a college project, with one twist: you control everything with just the mouse. Your character follows your cursor around the screen, and you click to shoot, dodging waves of enemies and bullets while trying to survive as long as you can.

Key Features

  • Mouse-only controls, your character follows the cursor and clicks to shoot
  • Bullet-hell waves of enemies and projectiles to dodge
  • A survival timer, see how long you can last
  • Fast, reflex-driven gameplay

Development Details

  • Language: C#

What I worked on

A solo college project where I set myself one rule: the whole game has to be playable with just the mouse. That constraint ended up shaping everything. What I built:

  • Mouse-driven movement, where the player character follows the cursor by projecting the mouse position into the world every frame
  • Click-to-shoot firing tied into a gun and bullet system
  • Survival timer system

Development Insights

I made this in college as a fun way to dig deeper into Unity and C#. I set the mouse-only rule on purpose to challenge myself and come up with something a little different, your character basically is the cursor. It went over well with my peers and instructors, and it really solidified how Unity's input and physics systems work for me.
