Project Overview

The Wild Wild East is a visual novel adventure game that takes players on a journey through a vibrant and chaotic world. Players will encounter a diverse cast of characters, each with their own stories and motivations. The game features a unique blend of humor and narrative depth, allowing players to explore the world and interact with its inhabitants in meaningful ways.

Key Features

  • Western setting with wacky characters
  • Unhinged humor and absurd scenarios
  • Amazing soundtrack that enhances the experience

Development Insights

This game was developed during a game jam, where I contributed to the inventory system and various other small aspects of the game. The project was a collaborative effort, and we aimed to create a unique gameplay experience that revolves around the theme of the Wild West. The game features a vibrant art style, engaging characters, and a humorous narrative that sets it apart from traditional visual novels. I learned a lot about collaborative game development and the importance of clear communication within a team as we worked in various time zones.
