Illuminagent
A Deep Dive

A game jam game I helped develop in Unity for a game jam.
You play as a person who is tasked with bringing light to a dark world filled with enemies.

Project Overview

In the cosmos, a realm of darkness threatens to engulf the universe. Players embody a radiant source of light. Tasked with illuminating dark planets, the Player embarks is sent to worlds to bring light to the universe and dispel the looming darkness.

Key Features

  • Unique light-based gameplay mechanics
  • Ability to choose your own difficulty
  • Challenging gameplay with replayability

Development Details

  • Engine: Unity
  • Language: C#
  • Status: Completed
  • Platforms: Web Browsers

Development Insights

This game was developed during a game jam, where I contributed to the enemy AI design and implementation. The project was a collaborative effort, and was ultimately ranked #51. The game features a unique mechanic where the player must navigate through dark environments, using light to reveal the town and defeat enemies. I learned a lot about collaborative game development and the importance of clear communication within a team.
