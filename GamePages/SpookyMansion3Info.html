Project Overview

Spooky Mansion 3 is a horror-themed game that immerses players in a haunted mansion filled with terrifying surprises. The objective is to navigate through the mansion, solve puzzles, and uncover the dark secrets hidden within its walls.

Key Features

  • Horror-like atmosphere
  • Randomized events and encounters
  • Weapon mechanics
  • Stealth elements

Development Insights

This game was developed during a game jam, where I contributed to the enemy AI mechanics. The project was left unfinished due to time constraints, but it showcases the potential for a horror game with randomized elements and stealth mechanics. The game features a spooky atmosphere and engaging gameplay that keeps players on their toes as they navigate through the haunted mansion.
