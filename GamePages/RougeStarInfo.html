Rouge Star
A Deep Dive

A stealth platformer game my team developed in Unreal Engine 4
while in college.

Project Overview

Rogue Star is a stealth platformer where you collect all the hidden treasure while sneaking past enemies and staying out of sight. It's got a distinct art style and leans hard into stealth, every level is about reading the enemies and slipping by without getting caught.

Key Features

  • Stealth gameplay built around hiding and sneaking past enemies
  • Enemy AI that reacts to both sight and sound
  • A detection system that punishes you when enemies notice you
  • Built from the ground up by a college team

Development Details

  • Role: Programmer (team project)
  • Engine: Unreal Engine 4
  • Language: C++/Blueprints
  • Status: Completed
  • Platforms: PC

What I worked on

This was my big college team project, and the one where I did the most work in C++ and Unreal. My focus was the camera and how the character moves and feels to control. What I handled:

  • The camera system that follows the player through the levels
  • The player controls, movement, turning, looking, and jumping
  • Setting up the character's animations
  • Helping a teammate with part of the enemy AI, the sight and sound detection that decides when you've been spotted

Development Insights

This was a collaborative project during college, and a big one. Getting to work in Unreal Engine 4 with C++ on a team taught me a lot, both about the engine itself and about splitting up work and staying coordinated when a bunch of people are building one game together. It's still one of the projects I'm most proud of.
