Rouge Star
A Deep Dive

A stealth platformer game my team developed in Unreal Engine 4
while in college.

Project Overview

Rouge Star is a stealth platformer game where players must collect all the hidden treasure while avoiding detection by enemies and using stealth mechanics to progress. The game features a unique art style, engaging gameplay, and a variety of challenges.

Key Features

  • Stealth mechanics that allow players to hide and sneak past enemies
  • AI driven behavior thats based on sight and sound
  • Built from the ground up

Development Details

  • Engine: Unreal Engine 4
  • Language: C++/Blueprints
  • Status: Completed
  • Platforms: PC

Development Insights

This project was a collaborative effort during my time in college. I focused on implementing the camera system, controls, and setting up animations. I also assisted a teammate with part of the AI system. This project helped me gain valuable experience in Unreal Engine 4 and teamwork in game development.
