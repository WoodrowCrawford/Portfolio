Pas de Deux: A Duet of Roses
A Deep Dive

A visual novel made for the Sealed With a Kiss game jam in Renpy.

Project Overview

As the lead programmer, I helped my team bring Pas de Deux: A Duet of Roses to life, a romance visual novel built in Ren'Py for the Sealed With a Kiss game jam. Can you find the secrets hidden among the trees of our enchanted forest?

Key Features

  • Branching choices that track which route and ending you're heading toward
  • A name-input system so the main character goes by whatever name you give them
  • Earlier choices are remembered and change how later scenes play out
  • A full cast with different expressions, plus voice acting

Development Details

  • Role: Lead Programmer (team project)
  • Engine: Ren'Py
  • Language: Python
  • Status: In Development (Demo Available)
  • Platforms: Windows, macOS

What I worked on

As the lead programmer, my job was taking the writing, art, and voice work the team made and wiring it all into a game that actually plays. The choice system is the part I'm happiest with. The main things I built:

  • The branching choice system, where your picks add up behind the scenes and decide which route and ending you get
  • The name input screen, including cleaning up what people type and falling back to a default if they leave it blank, then running that name through the whole script
  • Flags that remember earlier choices, so later scenes change based on what you did
  • The character sprites and expressions, transitions, and scene direction

Development Insights

This was a really fun collaborative project. Coming in as the lead programmer on a team of writers, artists, and voice actors taught me a lot about taking other people's work and pulling it all together into one finished thing. We built the demo for the jam and we're still adding to it.
