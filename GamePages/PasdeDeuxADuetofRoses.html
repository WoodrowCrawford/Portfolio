Development Insights

Creating "Pas de Deux: A Duet of Roses" was a collaborative effort for the Sealed With a Kiss game jam. The team focused on crafting a unique visual novel experience with engaging narrative elements and voice acting. The development process involved extensive use of Ren'Py and Python to bring the story and characters to life. As the lead programmer, I was responsible for implementing the game's mechanics and implementing the user interface. A demo of the game is available on itch.io, and we are currently working on further development to enhance the narrative and add more content.
