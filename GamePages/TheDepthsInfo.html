Project Overview

A game jam I worked on by myself where the theme was current. This game was made in a short time frame, focusing on the theme and gameplay mechanics. You play as a sailor who has to collect all the bottles in the the water while avoiding the enemies. This game is designed to be difficult and tedious, but also rewarding when you complete it.

Key Features

  • "Top down 2D based gameplay
  • Challenging gameplay experience
