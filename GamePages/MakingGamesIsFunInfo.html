Making Games is Fun!
A Deep Dive

A short visual novel I developed in Renpy for a school project.

Project Overview

Making Games is Fun! is a short visual novel about the joys and challenges of game development. It's told from a kid's perspective on purpose, since I made it to share why I love making games with a younger audience and wanted them to relate to it.

Key Features

  • A short, kid-friendly story about what it's like to make games
  • Walks through some of my own projects and experiences along the way
  • A simple, friendly art style aimed at a younger audience

Development Details

  • Engine: Ren'py
  • Language: Python
  • Status: Completed
  • Platforms: Windows, Mac, Web Browsers

Development Insights

I made this in Ren'Py for an English class project, where the goal was to pick a topic and a target audience to connect with. I chose game development, since I'm passionate about it and wanted to share what I love about it. My audience was kids, and a visual novel felt like the best way to show them why making games is fun, by handing them a game they could actually play. It taught me more about Ren'Py, and about how to get a message across while still keeping something fun to play.
