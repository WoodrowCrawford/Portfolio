A short visual novel I developed in Renpy for a school project.

Project Overview

Making Games is Fun! is a short visual novel that explores the joys and challenges of game development. It is told from a child's perspective, as I wanted them to be able to relate to the joys of game development.

Development Insights

I developed this game in Ren'py for an english class project. The goal was to think of a topic and a target audience to relate to. I chose the topic of game development, as I am passionate about it and wanted to share my experiences with others. My main target audience was kids, and I decided to develop a visual novel because I thought the best way to showcase to kids why making games is fun was to create a game that they could play and enjoy. Developing this game helped me to learn more about Ren'py and how to create a visual novel. I also learned how to create a game that is engaging and fun to play, while also conveying a message.
