The TV
A Deep Dive

A short visual novel horror game made in GB Studio.
You play as a boy who wants to watch TV, but all is not what it seems to be.

Project Overview

Play as a boy who only has one goal in mind; to watch TV. However, as you progress through the game, you will find that things are not as they seem. This short visual novel horror game is designed to be played in one sitting, offering a chilling narrative with unexpected twists.

Key Features

  • "Old school" visual style reminiscent of classic gameboy games
  • Never ending gameplay experience
  • Ability to be played on emulators
  • Chilling narrative with unexpected twists

Development Details

  • Engine: GB Studio
  • Language: GBScript
  • Status: Completed
  • Platforms: Gameboy emulators, Web Browsers

Development Insights

I created this project purely to learn GB Studio and to create a horror game that would be fun to play. The game is designed to be simple yet engaging, with a focus on atmosphere and storytelling. The project was completed in a short time frame, showcasing the capabilities of GB Studio for rapid game development.
